Coquito Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to create the perfect Coquito!

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Coconut Milk 2 cans
Sweetened Condensed Milk 1 can
Evaporated Milk 1 can
Cinnamon 1 tsp
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
White Rum 1 cup

Preparation Steps

  1. In a blender, combine coconut milk, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Add cinnamon and rum. Blend until smooth.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
  4. Serve chilled in small glasses, garnished with a sprinkle of cinnamon.
